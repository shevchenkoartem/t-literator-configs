Shyrokoyu dolynoyu mizh dvoma ryadkamy rozlozhystykh hir tykho teche po Vasyl'kivshchyni nevelychka richka Rastavytsya. Sered dolyny zeleniyut' rozkishni husti ta vysoki verby, tam niby potonulo v verbakh selo Verbivka. Mizh verbamy duzhe vyrazno y yasno blyshchyt' proty sontsya vysoka bila tserkva z tr'oma banyamy, a kolo neyi nevelychka dzvinytsya nenache zaplutalas' v zelenomu hilli starykh hrush. Podekudy z-pomizh verb ta sadkiv vyrynayut' bili khaty ta chorniyut' pokrivli vysokykh klun'.
